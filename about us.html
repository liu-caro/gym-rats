About Us.

This project was created at HackUMass VI by Caroline Liu and Jocelyn Chan. We were inspired by the difficult challenge that we have faced ourselves of trying to consistently go to the gym on a busy schedule. We are both second-year undergraduates attending Northeastern University. Caroline is a Computer Science and Design Major. Jocelyn is a Computer Science and Psychology Major. This was our first hackathon ever, and we are grateful for this experience because we learned so much:

  • Android App Studio
  • Android SDKs
  • HTML & CSS
  • Web APIs
  • Javascript
  • REST APIs
  • Perserverance & Patience
